The Big Bet Backfires: Addiction Crisis Hits Young Men Amid Sports Betting Boom

The expansion of online sports betting, once heralded as a win-win for state coffers and entertainment, is producing a darker legacy: a burgeoning public health crisis, particularly among young men. As mobile apps proliferate and in-game promotions intensify, the risk of gambling addiction has grown alarmingly — and research increasingly points to young male bettors as the group most vulnerable to harm.
